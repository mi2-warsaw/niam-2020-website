About NIAM 2020 Workshop

Warsaw, 22-24 April 2020

The aim of the workshop is to integrate scientific communities interested in Navigated, Interpretable, and Automated Machine Learning. NIAM is a place for discussion and learning the most recent advances in the above areas.
The essence of the workshop are poster sessions preceded by short (3-5 min) flash talks and also small group activities and discussions. To enhance networking and exchange of experience, we plan to focus this event on specific areas of ML and to keep it relatively small (around 30 participants).

The subject areas of NIAM include, but are not limited to:
- Navigated Machine Learning,
- Interpretable Machine,
- Automated Machine Learning,
- Automated Machine Learning,
- Evidence-Based Machine Learning.
